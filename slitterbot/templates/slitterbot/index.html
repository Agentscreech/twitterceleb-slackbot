Slack Bot that lets you chat with Twitter

To get this bot to run, you will need a twitter account and make a slack bot in your team.

Just give your bot a name and hit "Add bot integration" From there you will get an API Token. Copy that long string starting with "xoxb-"

Now you will have to invite your bot to a channel in your team's slack channel.

Once you have done all three things, press "Add new Bot"
